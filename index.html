About Us

PT Sinarkimia Utama (SKU) is one of Indonesia's respected manufacturers and distributors of basic and specialty chemicals, serving various industries. Established in 1996, the company now has offices in Jakarta, Surabaya and Medan.

With over 200 chemical products, SKU distributes to more than 3.000 customers across a wide spectrum of Indonesian industries. As a private company group that has successfully undertaken business in Indonesia, SKU strives to further enhance its performance, professionalism and technology.

This commitment allows it to provide the utmost service to its business partners and customers and to support the country's development in general. SKU is an evolving company that embraces change, understands trends and anticipates opportunities.
